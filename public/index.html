ALARM!

Your controller has entered an ALARM state! This locks the controller to ensure your safety.
[Option 1] Purge Queue and Clear Lockout

[Option 2] Ignore the error, Clear Lockout and Continue with Queue

Note: The safer option is Option 1. This is the safest method because it first clears the Queue, before unlocking the machine, avoiding any runaway moves etc. This aborts the job, but recovers the machine for the next task
You can opt to use Option 2, but NB note that depending on what caused the error, your machine have very likely lost its position (Failed probe, endstop hit, Gcode move not recognised etc) and continuing may be detrimental! Use this entirely at your own risk
